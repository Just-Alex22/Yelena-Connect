Yelena Connect lets you control your Linux PC from your Android phone over your local Wi-Fi network. No USB cable, no cloud account, no proprietary services required.

<b>Features</b>

* Remote keyboard — type text and send special keys to your PC
* Clipboard sync — bidirectional clipboard between phone and PC in real time
* Media controls — play, pause, skip tracks and adjust volume
* System monitor — real-time CPU, RAM and disk usage
* Process manager — view and terminate running processes
* App launcher — open installed applications remotely
* PC notifications — view your PC notifications on your phone
* File transfer — send files between devices
* QR code pairing — easy connection setup by scanning a QR code

<b>Requirements</b>

Requires the Yelena Connect desktop applet running on your Linux PC (available in the same repository). No account or internet connection needed — everything stays on your local network.
